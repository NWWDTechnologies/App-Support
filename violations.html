Violation Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. Purpose

This policy outlines behaviors and activities that are considered violations of our community standards and may result in warnings, suspensions, or bans from the app.

2. Prohibited Conduct

  • Harassment, bullying, or hate speech
  • Posting or sharing explicit, offensive, or violent content
  • Cheating, exploiting bugs, or manipulating app mechanics
  • Impersonation of other users, moderators, or developers
  • Spamming or distributing unsolicited advertising

3. Reporting Violations

We encourage all users to report misconduct through the support form. Please provide as much detail as possible so we can investigate appropriately.

4. Enforcement

Depending on the severity and frequency of violations, we may take actions including:

  • Warnings
  • Temporary account suspension
  • Permanent account ban

5. Appeal Process

Users who believe a violation decision was made in error may appeal by submitting a detailed request through the support form. We review each appeal case-by-case.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be posted here with a new effective date.
